Hello! I'm Alena Pearson

I’m currently a second-year student in the Multimedia Production program at Lethbridge College, where I focus on graphic design, photography, and web development. Throughout the program, I’ve learned how much I enjoy creating visuals—whether that’s designing logos, editing photos, or building websites from scratch.

I love combining creativity with technology. Web development has quickly become one of my favourite areas to explore, especially when it comes to creating clean, user-friendly designs that bring ideas to life.

After graduation, I hope to continue working in digital design and front-end development, helping small businesses build their branding, share their stories, and express their identity through strong visuals.
